# Typed Standards Specification — Royalty-Free Patent Statement

**Status: DRAFT — this statement takes effect when this file is merged to `main` by the maintainer. The maintainer's own act of merging the pull request that introduces this file constitutes execution of this statement.**

This statement is modeled on the royalty-free licensing goal of the W3C Patent Policy (§5) and the patent non-assertion covenant of the Open Web Foundation Agreement (OWFa 1.0). It is written in plain language. It is a real commitment by the Covenantor; it is not legal advice to anyone else.

## Definitions

- **Specification** — the Typed Standards Specification ([`docs/architecture/typed-standards-specification.md`](docs/architecture/typed-standards-specification.md) in this repository), in any version published in this repository or at its future canonical home (typedstandards.org), including the frozen historical drafts consolidated into it.
- **Essential Claims** — patent claims that the Covenantor owns or controls, now or in the future, that would necessarily be infringed by implementing the normative portions of the Specification. "Necessarily infringed" means there is no commercially reasonable non-infringing alternative for meeting the normative requirement.
- **Conformant Implementation** — an implementation of the Specification, in whole or of any conformance class it defines, that conforms to the Specification's normative requirements.
- **Covenantor** — Nathan Storey, the project maintainer, acting on his own behalf, with the intent that this covenant bind his successors and assigns with respect to the Specification.

## Covenant

To the extent of the patent rights the Covenantor owns or controls, the Covenantor irrevocably covenants not to assert any Essential Claims against any party for making, using, selling, offering for sale, importing, or distributing a Conformant Implementation.

This covenant is:

- **Royalty-free.** No fee, registration, reciprocal obligation, or other condition applies beyond what is stated in this document.
- **Scope-of-rights.** It covers Essential Claims the Covenantor owns or controls. It does not, and cannot, license anyone else's patents, and it makes no representation about third-party patent claims.
- **Worldwide and irrevocable**, except as provided under Defensive suspension below.

## Defensive suspension

If any party asserts a patent claim against the Covenantor, or against any Conformant Implementation, alleging that implementing the Specification infringes that party's patent, this covenant is suspended with respect to that party (and parties acting in concert with it) for as long as the assertion is maintained.

## What this statement does not do

- It grants no copyright or trademark rights. Copyright in the Specification is licensed separately under CC BY 4.0 (Specification §3); code in the project repositories is licensed under MIT (see [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md)).
- It does not warrant that Conformant Implementations are free of third-party patent claims.
- It is not legal advice.

## Contributions

Substantive contributions to the Specification are accepted on these same terms: by contributing normative text to the Specification, a contributor extends this same royalty-free covenant over the contributor's Essential Claims that are essential to the contributed text. See [IPR.md](IPR.md).

## Changes

Changes to this statement are made only through the project's ADR process (`docs/adr/`) and never reduce the rights granted for Specification versions published before the change.
